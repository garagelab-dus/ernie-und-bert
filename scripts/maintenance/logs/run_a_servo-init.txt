## Assumption

We need to evaluate the direction of rotation before setting target position.

If i set a mechanical minimum to a virtual position of 2048, 
and move gripper from minimum to maximum
and get min_pos = 2048 and max_pos = 3600,
then
- virtual target values below 2048 are impossible
- virtual targets higher then max_pos are impossible
- turning motor from 3600 over 4096 to 2050 is impossible.

Reason:
While turning from a high value to a low value over the maximum is possible in a circle, it's not possible in mechanical linear movements.
